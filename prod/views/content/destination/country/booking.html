Located in Andheri East, The Lalit Mumbai is a perfect starting point from which to explore Mumbai. The hotel offers a wide range of amenities and perks to ensure you have a great time. No matter what your reasons are for visiting Mumbai, The Lalit Mumbai will make you feel instantly at home.
