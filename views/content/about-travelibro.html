Every sigh at a sight, every smile in a picture, every finger that points and every silencing moment... every giggle that escaped, every heart that melted, every lump in the throat and every bear-hug that refused to go. Every strip of ticket and every stamp on the passport entail a story. We want to listen to yours and tell some of ours... let's talk!

Life is a journey full of memorable moments, those that we experience while we are travelling as well as the local activities we do in the city we live in. We seek to not only enable users to capture those precious memories but also inspire other like-minded travellers by sharing them and also relive them over and over again. Seamlessly connecting travel and social networking is what makes TraveLibro unique. We want to create a global community who capture, share and inspire others with their local and travel stories. Sharing these amazing journeys can inspire and fuel many aspiring souls plagued with wanderlust.

A travel social network like none other, TraveLibro aims to connect its users with other travellers, bloggers, agents along with being a meta-search engine for bookings such as flights, hotels, homestays, car rentals, and more. Besides, we are a perfect go-to for those who like to take the road less travelled. Each country and city (that we have covered on our portal currently) has a list of suggested must-dos, itineraries based on travel preference, hotels and restaurants, to sort you out. In short, we are heading towards creating a travel-ecosystem of sorts for our users.
